Services

No need to stress over the big (and small) details. Let us help you!

Partial Package

A partial package is usually for couples who need help with specific aspects of their wedding, but want to take on some of the planning themselves. This option is typically for those who have already started the planning process or have a clear vision but need expert assistance to ensure things run smoothly.

  • Consultation & Advice: Initial consultations to define your wedding style, vision, and budget. Advice on where to begin, what to prioritize, and timelines.
  • Vendor Recommendations: Help with selecting and recommending vendors (photographers, caterers, florists, etc.).
  • Design Assistance: Help with selecting a theme or color palette and basic advice on décor and style elements.
  • Budget Management: Providing budget guidelines and helping you stay on track with your spending.
  • Day-of Coordination: Limited coordination on the day of the wedding to ensure smooth operations.

Full Package

The full package is ideal for couples who want a completely hands -off approach and need a wedding planner to manage every detail from start to finish. This package allows the couple to relax and enjoy their engagement, knowing everything will be taken care of.

  • Full Wedding Planning & Design: Complete assistance with every step of the planning process, from choosing a venue to designing the event.
  • Vendor Coordination: Full vendor management, including selection, negotiation, and booking.
  • Budget Management: Ongoing budget management to ensure everything stays within the couple's desired budget.
  • Timeline & Scheduling: Creating a detailed wedding day timeline and managing all logistics.
  • Wedding Design & Décor: Full design work, including flowers, linens, seating arrangements, and more.
  • Guest List & RSVP Management: Help managing your guest list and seating charts.
  • Day-of Coordination: Full day-of coordination to ensure everything runs smoothly.
  • Emergency Kit: The planner will bring an emergency kit with supplies to handle any last-minute issues.
